# The future is open: digitalization, data and shared culture

![photo](/News/new-3/a.jpg)

The **industrialization of construction** cannot advance without a **deep digital transformation**. Technologies such as **BIM**, **artificial intelligence** or **digital component markets** are redefining the way we design, produce and manage buildings. The result? A **more efficient, more connected and more transparent** process.

From **CCiC**, we are committed to an **open and interoperable model**, where **data, designs and processes are shared** to generate **collective innovation**. We believe that creating a **common digital ecosystem** is the key to scaling the industrialized model and bringing it to the entire territory.

That's why we promote:

- **Digital transformation programs** adapted to the reality of the sector.
- **Support for advanced energy certification**, to build with Net Zero criteria.
- **Technology adoption** in small and medium enterprises, ensuring that no one is left behind.

Only in this way can we ensure that **industrialization is a structural and inclusive change**, capable of benefiting the entire sector, not just several actors.

The future of construction will be **collaborative or it will not be**.  
And from the **Catalonia Industrialized Construction Cluster**, we will continue working so that **Catalonia becomes an international reference** in a **new construction culture: open, digital, sustainable and shared**.
