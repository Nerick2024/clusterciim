# Building the future of housing is a collective challenge

![CCiC Team in front of Ca l'Alier](/News/new-1/a.jpg)

This is precisely what defines the mission of the **Catalonia Industrialized Construction Cluster**:

**Connecting companies + Knowledge + Innovation = Transforming the way we build today looking towards the future.**

From CCiC we have worked together with various partner companies to present a **strategic study for the Mexico City Council** on the initiative of **BitHabitat**, focused on the role of industry in new housing and rehabilitation. A project that poses **new challenges and prototypes** to advance towards more efficient, industrialized and sustainable models in the long term.

When different companies collaborate, the result is more than a project: it is **a new way of thinking about the city and the future of housing**.

Thanks to all the cluster companies that participate. Great #TeamCCiC!
